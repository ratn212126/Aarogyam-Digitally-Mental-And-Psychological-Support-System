About Mental and Psychological Disorder

Mental and psychological disorders affect how a person thinks, feels, manages stress, and interacts with others. We're here to provide support, understanding, and resources for individuals and families affected by these conditions.

Understanding

Learn about different types of mental and psychological disorders and how they affect daily life. These disorders encompass a range of conditions impacting emotions, behavior, stress management, and social functioning, which can significantly influence overall well-being and everyday activities.

Support

Access professional help, support groups, and community resources. you can consider accessing professional help, joining support groups, and utilizing community resources. For emotional distress, the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline offers 24/7 confidential support via call, text, or online chat.

Education

Educational resources and strategies for learning and development. A comprehensive learning and development (L&D) strategy outlines how an organization develops its workforce, aligning with business goals and addressing employee learning needs.

Community

Connect with others who understand and share similar experiences. Connecting with a community, especially one where individuals share similar experiences, is crucial for well-being. It fosters a sense of belonging, reduces feelings of isolation.

Mental and Psychological Conditions

Learning Disorder

Difficulties with mood regulation, behavior, stress management, or coping with emotions can be indicators of mental and psychological disorders, which are distinct from cognitive disabilities.


Learn More

Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety Disorder is a mental health condition characterized by excessive worry, fear, and nervousness, which can interfere with daily life, relationships, work, and overall functioning. It may persist over time and vary in intensity across different situations.

Learn More

Somatic Symptom Disorder

Somatic Symptom Disorder (SSD) is a psychological disorder involving excessive focus on physical symptoms like pain or fatigue, causing distress and affecting daily life and well-being.

Learn More

Memory Disorders

Memory problems can arise as a result of mental and psychological disorders, affecting concentration, recall, learning ability, daily functioning, and overall emotional well-being in individuals. Treatment focuses on engagement with people.

Learn More

Developmental Disorders

Developmental Disorders are neurological conditions affecting brain development, leading to impairments in skills like language, social interaction, or learning.They can impact everyday functioning and usually last throughout a person's lifetime.

Learn More

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) can develop as a consequence of experiencing or witnessing traumatic events, leading to intrusive memories, heightened anxiety, emotional distress, and difficulties in daily functioning.

Learn More

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) may develop as a result of mental and psychological factors, causing recurring intrusive thoughts, compulsive behaviors, anxiety, and difficulties in managing daily routines and decision-making.

Learn More

Sleeping Disorders

Sleep disorders, such as insomnia and frequent nightmares, are conditions that affect the ability to fall asleep, stay asleep, or achieve restful sleep. They can be caused by stress, mental and psychological factors, medical conditions, or lifestyle habits, leading to fatigue, irritability, and impaired daily functioning.

Learn More

Support Services


Professional Counselling

One-on-one therapy sessions with specialized therapists and counsellors can be highly beneficial for individuals with mental and psychological disorders. These sessions, often involving approaches like mindfulness practices and stress management techniques, aim to enhance emotional regulation, build resilience, improve coping strategies, and reduce symptoms of anxiety or depression. Therapists may also provide support in areas of relationship counseling, self-care planning and connecting individuals with community-based mental health resources.
Schedule Session

Support Groups

Guided group sessions and discussions can be highly beneficial for individuals with mental and psychological disorders, offering opportunities for emotional support, stress relief, and shared understanding in a safe environment. These sessions, often led by therapists or trained professionals, provide a structured space for individuals to express feelings, learn coping strategies, and build social connections through shared experiences.

Join Group
Educational Support

For individuals with mental and psychological disorders, effective support strategies and accommodations involve reducing stressors, providing clear and flexible instructions, incorporating relaxation or mindfulness techniques, and ensuring a safe and understanding environment. Accommodations should focus on promoting emotional well-being and adaptive coping, rather than only performance, to help individuals engage meaningfully in learning and daily activities.
